Company Overview

With an eye to the borderless world, Matex established its first overseas factory in Laguna, Philippines in 1997. At present, we have 100 machines and equipment for injection molding and die making. We undertake start-to-finish production from die making through injection molding to assembly. 24-hour mass production system enables us to respond customers' requirements flexibly and rapidly and to offer total solution. We have been enjoying a high evaluation from customers in fields which require high precision and high quality, such as OA equipment, automobile parts and electric appliance.
